Matcha Tiramisu With Raspberries

Description

Tiramisu is the perfect dessert to experiment and be creative with. You can create your own summer tiramisu with all kinds of fruit, but here we used raspberry and matcha (green tea powder). You can leave out matcha if desired and go full raspberry. Replace the matcha with a mixture of 3 tbsp and 1 /3 tbsp raspberry jam and 3 and ⅓ tbsp water and simply dip the ladyfingers in it as you would the matcha tea mixture

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp matcha powder

  • 400g raspberries

  • 2 egg whites

  • 90g confectioner sugar

  • 1tsp vanilla extract

  • 500g mascarpone cheese

  • 250ml water(warm)

  • 200g ladyfingers

Steps

  1. Add egg whites, confectioner’s sugar, and vanilla extract to a big bowl. Beat with a hand mixer with beaters until foamy. Add mascarpone to the bowl and mix until combined.

  2. Dissolve matcha powder in warm water and whisk vigorously with a matcha whisk. Let cool briefly.

  3. Dip half of the ladyfingers in the matcha tea and layer in the bottom of a baking dish. Spread over half of the mascarpone cheese mixture, then add half of the raspberries. Repeat with the rest of the ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese. Sieve an even, thin layer of matcha powder over the top and decorate with the remaining raspberries. Let chill in the fridge for at least 2 hrs., before slicing. Enjoy!
